Welcome to Gandhara Music Class

At Gandhara Music Class, we believe music is not just a skill but a way of life. Our mission is to provide high-quality training in different forms of music while preserving the rich cultural heritage of India.

We train students in both traditional and modern styles of music, helping them build confidence and creativity through performances and structured learning.

About



Pruthvi Hegde is a dedicated Hindustani classical vocalist who has successfully completed both Junior and Senior levels of training. The musical journey began under the guidance of Mrs. Kavyashree Hegde and is currently being nurtured by Shree Vinayaka Hegde Hirehadda.

Performed at prestigious venues such as Swarnavalli Mata, Shri Marikamba Temple, and Shri Siddhivinayaka Temple. Completed Prarambha, Praveshika Pratham, and Poorna Madhyama exams conducted by Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, Dharwad.

Founded Gandhara Music Class in Sirsi, Karnataka to guide young learners and spread the tradition of Hindustani music.

Courses Offered

  • Hindustani Classical
    Learn the foundation of Indian classical music, ragas, and traditional compositions with step-by-step guidance.
  • Light Music
    Training in bhajans, devotional songs, and semi-classical music to improve voice control and expression.
  • Film Music
    Explore popular film songs, playback techniques, and modern singing styles to enhance versatility.
